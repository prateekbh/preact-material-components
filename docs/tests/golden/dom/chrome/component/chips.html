Components and their props
  • Chips
    • choice true/false
      Makes chips selectable
    • filter true/false
      Makes chips multi-selectable
  • Chips.Chip
    • no specific props
  • Chips.Icon
    • leading true/false
      Optional. Indicates that it's a leading icon
    • trailing true/false
      Optional. Indicates that it's a trailing icon
  • Chips.Text
    • no specific props
